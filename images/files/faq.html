Frequently Asked Questions


1. What is TechPharm?
TechPharm is an AI-based healthcare platform designed to make access to medicines and medical support easier, faster, and more efficient for everyone.

2. How does TechPharm work?
Our platform uses AI to guide users through finding nearby pharmacies, checking medicine availability, and receiving basic health advice in a user-friendly way.

3. Is TechPharm a pharmacy?
No, TechPharm is not a pharmacy. We connect users with licensed pharmacies and provide AI-powered support for better health decisions.

4. Do I need a prescription to use TechPharm?
Yes, for certain medicines a valid prescription is required. Our platform can help guide you on how to upload or share your prescription securely.

5. Is TechPharm free to use?
Yes, TechPharm’s basic services are completely free. We aim to make healthcare support accessible to everyone.

6. Is my personal information safe with TechPharm?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and data privacy practices to ensure your information is secure.

7. Can I talk to a doctor through TechPharm?
Coming soon! We’re working on integrating a feature where you can consult certified medical professionals directly through our platform.

8. Which areas does TechPharm currently serve?
We are initially focusing on select locations in India, but we plan to expand soon. Stay tuned for updates!
